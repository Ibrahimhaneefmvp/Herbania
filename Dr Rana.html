Meet Dr. Naushad Ali Rana

A dedicated guardian of nature's healing secrets in Unani medicine.

Professional Profile

Dr. Naushad Ali Rana is a distinguished Unani physician with over 15 years of specialized experience in holistic healing. Based in Delhi at Hamdard Wellness Center, he blends ancient Unani wisdom with modern preventive care, focusing on natural remedies derived from plants and herbs to restore bodily balance.

As Chief Physician at Hamdard Laboratories India, Dr. Rana has treated thousands, emphasizing immunity boosting—especially during COVID-19—through Unani's natural approaches. He is registered with the Bhartiya Chikitsa Parishad and speaks English and Hindi fluently.

Education and Expertise

  • BUMS (Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery) from Aligarh Muslim University (2006).
  • MD in Unani Preventive & Social Medicine from Aligarh Muslim University (2012).
  • 2 years teaching experience in Unani medical colleges, including courses on Unani introduction and Hijamah (cupping therapy).
  • Specializes in preventive medicine, holistic wellness, and integrating Unani with contemporary health practices.

Contributions and Achievements

Dr. Rana has represented Hamdard in international events, such as Ayurveda Day 2020, highlighting Unani's role in immunity enhancement. He serves as In-charge of the Medical Board at Hamdard and contributes to research on natural therapies for chronic conditions like digestive disorders and stress-related ailments.
